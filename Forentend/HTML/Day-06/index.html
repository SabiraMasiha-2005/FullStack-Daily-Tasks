Welcome to Cyber Shield

Cyber security is the practice of protecting computers, mobile devices, networks and personal information from hackers, malware and cyber criminals.

Every day millions of people become victims of online attacks because they are unaware of cyber threats. This website helps you understand common cyber attacks and teaches simple ways to stay safe online.

"Think Before You Click!"

About Cyber Security


Cyber security protects internet users from digital attacks.

It helps keep personal information, banking details, passwords, emails, social media accounts and company data safe.

Why is Cyber Security Important?

  • Protects Personal Data
  • Prevents Online Fraud
  • Stops Hackers
  • Protects Bank Accounts
  • Secures Business Information
  • Protects Mobile Phones
  • Keeps Social Media Accounts Safe

Common Cyber Attacks

Attack Description
Phishing Fake emails or websites that steal your username and password.
Malware Harmful software that damages your computer.
Ransomware Locks your files and asks for money.
Identity Theft Criminals use your personal information.
Password Attack Hackers guess or steal passwords.
